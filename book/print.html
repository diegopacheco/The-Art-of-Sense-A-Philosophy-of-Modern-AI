Chapter ZERO

Why I wrote this book?

In the summer of 2024, I expend the whole summer learning about AI. I did 300+ coding POCs, I read several books, I did several courses. The of 2025, I test all AI coding agents I could, I wrote MCPs, I did several experiments.

There is some much out there, there is some many tools and people saying many things. This book should be a compass to give you in your AI journey for engineering.

Chapter 1 - Making sense of AI

First of all we need make sense of AI. What is it? How does it work? What are the different types of AI? In this chapter we will explore these questions and lay the foundation for understanding AI.

AI it's 2 steps forward and one step back. AI Halucinates, AI ignore your requests, AI makes mistakes. We need to understand the limitations of AI and how to work around them.

Them we need to understand how AI is changing how we do engineering. What are the new ways of working, what are the non-obvious ways we can leverage AI to be more productive and effective engineers.

Reality

Even with all the problems and the scams. AI is real and AI is here to stay. IDK If AGI will happen one day, maybe, maybe not but there is indication we are close to AGI.

Don't be fooled by markething of people saying I have AGI, people lie, and LLM models do not think, even they say "thinking".

Now we dont need AGI in order to have value from AI. The reality is that AI is a force of disruption, some as we saw with the internet and mobile phones.

Therefore change is happening, and you need to adapt to it. You can either be a victim of change or you can be the driver of change.

Ramdoness

AI is not precise and not 100% reproduceble. Does not matter how goood your prompt is, AI will generate different outputs. AI is really random in nature. Cory Doctorow and his amazing post: LLMs are slot-machines cleverly explain the random nature of LLM outputs. IF you need 100% precision, Generative AI is not for you, LLMs are not for you.

Generative AI just trying to predict the next sequence of tokens. That's all, a auto-complete on asteroids :-). You need understand the ramdon nature of Gen AI when using LLMs.

Fooled By AI

IF AI is lying to you, how do you know?

Think about that. If you don't know anything AI is "talking about", basically you are in deep trouble. You must be able to validate, fact check and even disagreen with AI output. High dependency on AI is bad, we should be able to program if we dont have internet or are running out of tokens.

Do you know how to know when a human using AI is fooling you? Do you know if you are fooling yourself with AI?

Take a look on the Clever Hans effect.

The dark side of AI

AI can and is used for a lot of scans. AI and people using AI can fool you in many ways. AI has a darkside. Perhaps many dark sides.

Outsorcing Gyn Workout

Think about this, we could easily pay someone to go to the gum for us. That person can do push ups, run, lift weights. Be on the gym everyday and at the same time never be on the gym. Genius! Well the issue is that your "contractor" will be strong and in shape but you will not. Altought you can claim the glory of going to the gym, you do not have the benefits, you only have status.

With AI is the same thing. Anyone can use AI to generate code, does that mean we are done there? Thing about it, everyboddy has the same AI, same models, same capabilitites. So just using AI is not differentiating.

Solutions vs Wrappers

Now, every single startup is building or vibing something with .ai or a new Agentic. Unther the hood most of the time is just a wrapper arounf OpenAI API or Anthoropic API. So what is the real value add?

I love Rappers but I never like Wrappers. In my book Principles of software architecture modernization I wrote about the dangerous of wrappers with internal shared libraries. Now we have the same danger but with external tools. There is a explosion of trools, new tools, all old tools being rebranded to ai, or ai features popping up in softwares we would not expect.

Vibe Coding

Andrej Karpathy coined the tern "vibe coding" in one of his tweets on FEB 2025.

Vibe coding is a pratice where you generate prompts and you do not look at the code. You assume AI will take care of everything. However that preisse is wrong. AI does not get all right. AI:

  • Ignore you requests: You ask for Java 25, AI deliver java 21.
  • AI Halucinates: And make up API that don't exist or code that give you bugs at runtime.
  • AI Just ignores you: You ask for a specific library, AI just ignore it and use something else.

Vibe code is very like try-and-error, you can build games with it even but dont foll yourself you cant use vibe coding for all the things. Not reading the code and not reading the DIFFs is a awful pratice. Asking for randon changes and hoping the bugs go away is not a startegy. Any serious software engineer with a brain will tell you that vibe coding is a bad idea.

Where you can use vibe coding

  • It's a small utility: It's very small, you would not do it, and it's not your core business. Example: A small script to convert CSV to JSON.
  • It's for throw away discovery: You are prototyping something very fast, and you will throw away the code later.
  • It's for learning: You are learning a new language or framework, and you want to get a feel for it quickly.

Considering that use cases, vibe coding is fine. However I would need say that for the last one you want read the code. Therefore is not vibe coding. Now if you want know is possible to do this? And you are just aiming for pheasability yes, you can vibe code it but throw away after and do it right.

Wehere you MUST NOT use vibe coding

To the point where you should ban vibe coding. Your CORE business should be be vibe coded. Cive coding your core business is a recipe for disaster. IF one day we got AGI them we can revisit this. One important not, people lie, and they will market anything as "AGI", so we might have AGI but it might now be what you think it will be. Meaning we still have jobs, this idea that AI will kill engineers jobs is absurd. Thanks God at this point people are getting into sense as the hype cycle with AI is going down.

Your CORE business is how you make money, it's your bread and better. Who wants to kill your milk cow? No one. Therefore don't vibe code your core business.Because that's a recipe for disaster.

Vibe Payments

Vibe Coding to some degree is a lack of respect. It's a lack of respect for people entires carrears and hard working years in this profession. Vibe coding means you are not paying attention into the code. Think about this, if AI is doing everything, and you are not paying attention on the code, how much you should be payed? Or should you be payed the same thing every month? IMHO we need Vibe Payments. Vibe Coders should be payed with vibe payments. It would look like this:

  • One month you get 50% less
  • Another month you get 30% less
  • Next month you get 20% more
  • Another month is no payments
  • Next time you own -240% of your salary

You would not like it. But that did not stop you throw bad code on others. My friends belive me AI generates a lot, a lot of thrash code. We need to do better.

AI Input

People see AI as ouput. As a way to "Beef Up" engineers. It's not wrong but you cant't just use AI and never look the code, this is recipe for disaster.

AI can be great teacher, however we need to always rember the teacher makes mistakes too. Using AI for input still a great advantage, you preserve the output to be yours.

A tale of High School

When I was in the high school. I rember a teacher of mine saying that people would goto google.com and just search for past year class and find the same homework and download a power point presentation and simple change they name on it. Of couse this would never work because the teach was smarter and usually people doing that took ZERO.

Using AI just for output, "vibe coding" it's shuting down your brain and we are not evern close to have AGI. So thisis a recipe for disaster. IMHO is not different than copying someones homework and just putting your name on it.

Now ofcouse, people will use internet and it's okay to be "inspired" by somebody else work, no one start from scrath but you need spend the time and review and change it.

Lack of Respect

It's lack of respect to throw some code into someone to review when the reviwer is the first person to read it, before even the "maker" or to be more honest the "viber".For this reason and 100 other reasons you need read code AI produces.

You + AI

Now if you use AI for input and do the due dilligence process, them you actually are doing this right, you are using ai for input but the output is yours, it's your code, your work, must have your finger prints, that way we do best of both worlds. You learn, you produce and you grow.
